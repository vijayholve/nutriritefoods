Work culture
At HyFun we believe that our workforce is the backbone of our business and our people are central to our growth strategy. We are a young group with ambitious plans of exponential growth in the years to come and thus we offer a stimulating work environment where new ideas are encouraged and hard work is rewarded. We believe in attracting the right talent, grooming them into our culture and providing a proper stimulus for performance through a transparent and robust reward and recognition system. At HyFun we believe that work is worth celebrating and we therefore nurture a collaborative and an engaging work culture. Whether it’s festivals or socials outings, we celebrate it all.
